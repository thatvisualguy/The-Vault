a suggestion.

✅ Always define

Approved backgrounds

Typography

Wrong font =
wrong feeling.

Typography sets tone faster than copy does. One heading font. One body font. Defined sizes. No exceptions.

Heading Font
Personality · Impact · Recall
Body Font — readable, clear, trustworthy at any size.
Readability · Trust · Clarity
🚫
Always include: a banned fonts list. Comic Sans lives rent-free on too many pitch decks.

Brand voice

What you say matters.
How you say it matters more.

Same message, different voice = completely different brand. Define it. Document it. Make it impossible to get wrong.

Professional, not stiff Direct, not rude Warm, not cheesy Bold, not arrogant

Include: sample copy, words you use, words you avoid, and tone shifts for different contexts (ads vs. support vs. social).
